Adaptive Hybrid Architecture for NRIQA

Maddel, A., Patil, R., Bhavsar, T., Gajbahar, A., Jaiswal, S., Joshi, P.
IEEE · ETFI 2026 · Pune, India · 12–14 February 2026

Proposes a dynamic routing pipeline for No-Reference Image Quality Assessment that avoids full-ensemble execution. A lightweight gradient-boosted classifier selects the optimal IQA model from a diverse pool of traditional (BRISQUE, NIQE) and deep-learning (MANIQA, HyperIQA) algorithms based on low-cost perceptual features — achieving performance competitive with the best individual model at a fraction of the computational cost.
